How much does your photo retouching really cost?

A common mistake when calculating the cost of retouching an image is basing only on from who the customers are and how many requirements they ask for. In fact, valuing a photo is not so simple. The cost includes not only the salary paid to the photo editors but also a host of costs, both direct and indirect: health insurance, computers, office space, HR, time to complete a picture editing, etc.
