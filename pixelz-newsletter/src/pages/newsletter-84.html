PIXELZ NEWS

Great News from Platform Team

New Pricing Page

Speed is the most important thing that many customers require from a photo retouching service. Therefore, they will choose the partner that can fulfill their orders quickly, even when they have to pay a bit more money. That is the reason why reducing time is the most important thing that your service strategy should accomplish.

Change for innovation, creating more gadgets to make things easier and more convenient to customers. Moreover, it saves time and money for customers, allowing customers to manually select and retrieve photos anytime they want.

Launching a new quote site is very important. It not only dramatically improve and simplify the information we provide about what is included in our plans and the difference between them but also allows potential customers to see the exact price they would pay for specific retouching tasks of different service packages. This makes it easy for customers to select the right service packages and feel more satisfied.

This event was launched along with the launch of the Solo package’s new price and our two pause plans which are Solo Pause and Professional Pause.

New SOLO Subscription Plan

The new Solo package requires any customer, including regular customers, to choose the paid subscription to use our monthly service. Because we are having the advantage of using SaaS to replace the old business model. That means we only work with customers that intend to use our services regularly. It is even happier to hear that there are 36 more SOLO customers registered successfully.

Pause Plans

One of the major insights from the churn investigation conducted in Spring 2018 was that up to 25% of the churn on Professional was due to seasonal demand. Customers who were perfectly happy with Professional were simply downgrading to Solo and then upgrading again when they had more images.

We wanted to remove these customers from our churn numbers so we created the option for both Solo and Professional subscribers to pause their subscriptions for just $4 per month. We also created the option for customers to cancel and delete their accounts completely.

This makes it possible to find out which customers really want to work with us so that we have more time focused on potential customers and those who want to cooperate with us for a long time. In addition, it will help the CS team work more efficiently.

Besides, one of the success in July was that We successfully converted the oldest Legacy's account into Solo Free and now there is no longer any Legacy accounts on the system.
